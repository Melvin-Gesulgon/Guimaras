How to Get to Guimaras

From Iloilo City, you can either ride through the RoRo (Roll on Roll Off) sea vessel in Lapuz, Muelley Looney, Iloilo City or through the much faster and lighter ferry boat in Parola, Iloilo. The ferry boat will take you to Guimaras in about 15 minutes. That's almost twice the speed of the bulkier RoRo vessel. But, if you're planning to explore Guimaras in your car or motorcycle, the RoRo vessel would make it happen for you. At the time of writing, the fare is 40 Pesos for a one way trip.

Quick Tips in Visiting the Island Paradise of Guimaras

Once you reach the Jordan Wharf, go to the Tourism Assistance Center booth. From there, the friendly staff can guide you to a perfect itinerary to suit your needs. I would highly suggest that you rent a van if you're looking to tour the island without sweating it out. Temperature during the day could be punishing, especially, if it's summer time. But, if you're the adventurous type and would rather experience firsthand how the locals travel, you can either travel through a tricycle, motorcycle or a jeepney.
